Description

Survival Cow is an exciting game of evil Ufos.

Run while you are being chased by a dangerous Ufo. Dodge laser rays shot by the spaceship. Take advantage of the abducting ray to jump over obstacles but be careful, they may catch you!

About

Survival Cow was made by Copito System, sibblings from Argentina. It will be available on the App Store and Google Play soon, with a free preview for Windows, Mac OS X and Linux. It was built using the awesome, free and open-source game framework, LÖVE.
